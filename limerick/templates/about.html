Discover Limerick's History

Limerick is a city steeped in history, with landmarks like the 13th-century King John's Castle and St. Mary's Cathedral, which dates back to the 12th century. The city's history is enriched by its Viking heritage and the role it played during the Irish Revolutionary period. Visitors can explore the fascinating blend of ancient and modern elements throughout the city.

Cultural Highlights

Limerick is not only a city of history but also a hub of culture.
