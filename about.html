About Discover Limerick

Welcome to Discover Limerick, your go-to guide for exploring the beautiful city of Limerick, Ireland. Whether you're a first-time visitor or a seasoned traveler, we aim to make your experience unforgettable with curated tours, local insights, and tips on the best places to visit, stay, eat, and explore in Limerick.

Our Mission

Our mission is to showcase the best of Limerick to the world. From the rich history of King John's Castle and the picturesque beauty of the River Shannon, to the modern vibrancy of the city, we help you uncover the hidden gems that make Limerick one of Ireland's most unique destinations.

Why Choose Us?

  • Expert Local Knowledge: Our team consists of locals who are passionate about their city and eager to share it with visitors.
  • Tailored Experiences: We offer customized tours to suit your interests, whether it's history, nature, food, or culture.
  • Comprehensive Guides: Get access to detailed maps, guides, and recommendations to help you navigate the city with ease.

Our Team

The Discover Limerick team is made up of passionate locals who have a deep love for the city. Our team includes experienced tour guides, travel experts, and cultural enthusiasts, all working together to make your visit to Limerick truly special.

Join Us on Our Journey

We believe that travel is more than just sightseeing—it's about connecting with people, cultures, and stories. Let us guide you through the heart of Limerick, where history meets modern charm, and every street tells a story. Explore, experience, and enjoy all that Limerick has to offer with us!
